
# Retail Customer Behavior Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail customer shopping behavior using **Python, SQL,  and Power BI**. The goal is to transform raw transaction data into actionable business insights that help improve customer engagement, optimize marketing strategies, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

A retail company wants to better understand customer purchasing behavior by analyzing customer demographics, shopping patterns, discounts, subscriptions, product ratings, and purchasing trends. The objective is to identify opportunities to increase sales, improve customer loyalty, and optimize business strategies.

---

## 📊 Dataset Information

- **Total Records:** 3,900
- **Columns:** 18
- **Features Include:**
  - Customer Demographics
  - Purchase History
  - Product Categories
  - Review Ratings
  - Discounts
  - Shipping Type
  - Subscription Status
  - Purchase Frequency

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Female customers generated slightly higher revenue than male customers.
- Subscribers spent more on average than non-subscribers.
- Express shipping customers had higher purchase values.
- Loyal customers contributed significantly to total revenue.
- Discounts positively influenced customer purchases.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Expand loyalty programs.
- Increase subscription benefits.
- Optimize discount strategies.
- Promote top-rated products.
- Focus marketing on high-value customer segments.
